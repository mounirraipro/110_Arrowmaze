# Arrow Maze

Arrow Maze is a free browser puzzle where every arrow matters. Study the grid, pick the safest pieces first, and clear the board without letting one rushed move spoil the route.

## Copy Direction

Keep the page practical, human, and suitable for a general audience. Avoid official-app claims, guaranteed rewards, real-money claims, misleading ownership language, and pressure to install anything.

## Core Play Advice

Start with arrows that point toward open space, then work inward once the board is less crowded.
